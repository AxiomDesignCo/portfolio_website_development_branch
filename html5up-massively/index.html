In addition to an excellent website, Axiom Design provides a vast array of benefits to our clients:

Management of your own website - we use both WordPress Content Management Systems and HTML5 templates to complete our websites. Purchase of a website includes 2 - 1 hour training sessions so you can update your website at your leisure. Also, you can call any one of our designers for support. If you prefer a hands-off approach, you can select one of our fairly-priced support packages and website hosting services.

Meet Face-to-Face - our office is in Media, Pennsylvania, right in the heart of Delaware County. If you prefer to meet elsewhere, we can come to you!

Fairly-Priced, Organized, and Efficient - You can have a website up and running within a few days. Additionally, we grant access to Project Management Software so you can supply us with tasks and check the progress of those tasks in real-time.

Search Engine Optimization — Make your site show up on the first page of Google results when a user searches for your company, products, or services. Additionally, we specialize in pay per click advertising to direct even more traffic to your site. We’re constantly updating and monitoring your campaign to make sure your internet marketing is the best it can be.

Social Media Updates - Many businesses are too busy to keep up with their website and social media accounts. We can offer a social media package where we would update your accounts on a daily, weekly, monthly, or quarterly basis.
